# METADOC: Architectural Typology & Research Standards

## 1. Ontological Foundation
All research within the ORGANVM ecosystem is governed by the principles of **Metatheoretical Synthesis** and **First Principles Reconstruction**. We do not summarize; we engage in **Archetypal Systems Analysis** to extract the "Deep Structure" (the eternal essence) beneath the "Surface Structure" (temporal style), establishing invariant "Covenants" for creation.

Our framework traverses three temporal pillars:
1. **Historiography (The Past):** What was.
2. **Ontology (The Present):** What is.
3. **Teleology (The Future):** What must be.

We operate as authors of a **Paradigm**, creating Transformative Systems designed to actively force the future into a specific, resilient shape.

---

## 2. Pillar I: Historiography (The Past)

### A. Transdisciplinary Isomorphism & Pattern Language
We study history to identify the invariant patterns (Alexanderian Method) that must be present for a system to be functional. We search for isomorphisms across different fields and eras to build our Covenants.

### B. Genealogical Inquiry (Foucauldian Approach)
We conduct a "history of the present" to understand the power structures and logic that led to a current form's failure, allowing us to alchemize a path that breaks the cycle.

### C. First Principles Deconstruction
The "Alchemy" of our process:
*   **Thesis:** The historical "Form" (What was).
*   **Antithesis:** The current "Failure/Gap" (What is).
*   **Synthesis:** The "New Covenant" (What must be).

---

## 3. Pillar II: Ontology (The Present - Lifecycle Validation)
Research is a continuous loop synchronized with the product lifecycle to validate the present state.

1.  **Pre-Alpha (Discovery):** Market validation, concept testing, and white-space identification.
2.  **Alpha & Beta (Validation):** Internal technical stability and external user feedback/usability.
3.  **Live/Growth (Market Post-Launch):** Measuring penetration, CX, and identifying new segments.

### The "5 Cs" Framework for Synthesis
Every analysis must apply: **Cite**, **Compare**, **Contrast**, **Critique**, and **Connect**.

---

## 4. Pillar III: Teleology (The Future - Strategic Foresight)
To move from historical synthesis to concrete future execution without drifting into speculative fiction, we employ **Prospective Theory** and **Strategic Foresight**.

### A. Environmental Scanning (STEEP)
Map the Social, Technological, Economic, Environmental, and Political drivers. Track "Weak Signals" and "Megatrends" rather than guessing.

### B. Scenario Planning & Transition Design
Build 3-4 plausible futures based on critical uncertainties. Stress-test our Covenants and Products against these scenarios to identify necessary "Pivots and Dodges." Treat the shift from the current Regime to the preferred future Landscape as a design problem.

### C. Backcasting (The Concrete Plan)
Start with the "Omega" (desired future state) and work backward to the present. Generate a high-fidelity Roadmap detailing the infrastructure and covenants required at specific temporal milestones to reach the Omega.

### D. Causal Layered Analysis (CLA)
Analyze the future across four depths:
1.  **Litany:** Surface-level trends (the news).
2.  **Social Causes:** Systemic drivers (economics/policy).
3.  **Worldview:** Deep-seated beliefs supporting the system.
4.  **Myth/Metaphor:** The unconscious stories (the "Alchemy") that, when changed, alter the future form.
