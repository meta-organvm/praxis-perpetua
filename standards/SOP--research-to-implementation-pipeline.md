# SOP: Research-to-Implementation Pipeline (The Gold Path)

## 1. Ontological Purpose
This SOP defines the post-research transformation process. It ensures that raw market intelligence is not merely "stored" but is systematically processed through bibliography, synthesis, and alchemy, culminating in actionable technical specifications.

**Mandatory Form & Content:** All documents produced via this SOP must adhere to the standards defined in `METADOC--research-standards.md`.

**Upstream inputs:** `SOP--source-evaluation-and-bibliography.md` (evaluated sources, annotated bibliography), `SOP--typological-hermeneutic-analysis.md` (Covenants, pattern language). **Downstream:** `SOP--strategic-foresight-and-futures.md` (consumes scenarios and backcasted roadmaps at Stage III-IV).

---

## 2. Stage I: Annotated Bibliography (Historiography / The Past)
**Goal:** Create a high-fidelity record of all sources gathered during the Discovery Phase.

*   **Structure:** List every URL, paper, interview, or data dump.
*   **Annotation Requirement:** For each source, provide:
    *   **Trust Score:** (1-10) based on source reputation.
    *   **The "Nugget":** The single most valuable finding from this source.
    *   **Keywords:** Tags for cross-referencing in Stage II.
*   **Genealogical Inquiry:** Trace the "history of the present" for each competitor to understand the power structures and logic that led to their current form's failure.
*   **Theorization Note:** If a source is opaque (e.g., closed-source proprietary logic), use the bibliography to list *theories* of its operation to discover deep structure isomorphisms.

---

## 3. Stage II: Synthesizing Document (Ontology / The Present)
**Goal:** Aggregate individual competitor "Meat" and "Gaps" into a unified market view for continuous lifecycle validation.

*   **The Matrix:** Create a CSV or Markdown table mapping all analyzed targets against project-specific Core Drives.
*   **The "5 Cs" Application:** Apply Cite, Compare, Contrast, Critique, and Connect to every target in the matrix.
*   **First Principles Deconstruction:** Deconstruct the historical forms down to their fundamental truths (Thesis vs. Antithesis).
*   **Pattern Language:** Observe recurring problems and the forms that successfully solved them to identify invariant patterns.

---

## 4. Stage III: Alchemizing Document (Teleology / The Future)
**Goal:** Engage in Strategic Foresight and Conceptual Innovation through Integration.

*   **The Transmutation (Synthesis):** Take the historical "Form" (What was) and the current "Failure" (What is) to forge the "New Covenant" (What must be).
*   **Environmental Scanning (STEEP):** Ensure the transmutation accounts for Social, Technological, Economic, Environmental, and Political drivers.
*   **Scenario Planning:** Stress-test the new covenants against 3-4 plausible future scenarios to identify necessary Pivots and Dodges.
*   **Causal Layered Analysis (CLA):** Ensure the new paradigm addresses all four depths: Litany, Social Causes, Worldview, and Myth/Metaphor.

---

## 5. Stage IV: Implementation Specifications (Backcasting)
**Goal:** Create high-precision, concrete roadmaps and blueprints from the desired future state (Omega) backward to the present.

*   **Feature Blueprints:** Break the "Alchemy" down into specific modules that act as intentional interventions (Transition Design).
*   **Verification Requirements:** Define exactly how truth is confirmed for this module.
*   **Security & Privacy Specs:** Detail the local-only or ZKP requirements.
*   **Actionable Issues:** Direct translation of specs into GitHub Issues or JIRA tickets with clear acceptance criteria.

---

## 6. Execution Flow
1. **Annotate:** Complete within 24 hours of Finishing Landscape Research.
2. **Synthesize:** Map the Matrix.
3. **Alchemize:** Brainstorm the Golden Path and apply Strategic Foresight.
4. **Specify:** Backcast the Blueprints to the dev-backlog.
