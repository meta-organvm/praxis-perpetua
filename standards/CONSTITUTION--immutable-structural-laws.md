# CONSTITUTION: Immutable Structural Laws of the ORGANVM System

**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Date:** 2026-03-20 | **Status:** ENACTED
**Authority:** Vigiles Aeternae — Agon Cosmogonicum (Constitutional consensus)
**Enforced by:** Ordo Seraphorum (Seraphim) | **Recorded by:** Ordo Testium Aeternum (Witnesses)

---

## Preamble

These laws are not conventions, preferences, or guidelines. They are **immutable structural laws** — truths so fundamental that every governance philosophy in the Agon independently confirms them. They were derived from the intersection of the metaLAWs codex (100+ universal laws across 9 domains), the founding Covenants, and the first Agon audit cycles.

A Constitutional law can be enacted when ALL active regimes agree on a finding across at least two Agon cycles. These founding laws were derived from the universal structures that all nine founding regimes share — the invariants that hold regardless of whether you view the system through Tolkien's delegated authority or Malazan's pressure accumulation.

**These laws cannot be amended by any single agent, session, or regime. Amendment requires a full Agon cycle with supermajority + Cosmogonist ratification.**

---

## Article I: Identity Must Be Declared

Every entity in the system — repo, organ, module, agent — must declare what it is and what it does. This declaration (seed.yaml, README, CLAUDE.md) is the entity's **contract with the system**. Existence without declaration is permitted temporarily (the bench, the incubator), but promotion requires declaration.

**Source:** Architects (Ordo Architectorum). **MetaLAW:** L1 Causality, B1 Identity.

## Article II: Records Are Immutable

Once an event is recorded — in the Chronicle, in git history, in ontologia events, in session transcripts — it cannot be edited, deleted, or retroactively altered. The record is the ground truth. Correction is achieved by appending new records that reference and supersede the old, never by modifying the old.

**Source:** Witnesses (Ordo Testium Aeternum). **MetaLAW:** L1 Causality, D6 As Above So Below.

## Article III: Transition Requires Readiness, Not Permission

No entity is promoted because someone declares it ready. Promotion occurs when the entity demonstrates readiness through evidence: passing tests, satisfying governance criteria, surviving audit. The Psychopomps guard the gate, but they judge readiness, not worthiness.

**Source:** Psychopomps (Ordo Psychopomporum). **MetaLAW:** A5 Natural Selection, L7 Compensation.

## Article IV: Every Power Must Declare Its Failure Mode

No entity, regime, order, tool, or process is exempt from declaring how it could fail. The Phaethon principle is Constitutional: every power carries its own antidote in the form of a declared blind spot, a named corrective, and a failure progression. Power without declared failure mode is not power — it is denial.

**Source:** All Orders (unanimous). **MetaLAW:** L3 Entropy, F3 Polarity.

## Article V: No Single Entity Owns Truth

Truth is distributed. The registry, the seed graph, the organism, the ontologia, the chronicle — each holds a facet. No single source is authoritative in isolation. The system's truth emerges from the convergence of multiple independent sources.

**Source:** All Regimes (derived from divergence analysis). **MetaLAW:** L6 Connection, C6 Information Wants To Be Free.

## Article VI: Entropy Is The Default; Order Requires Energy

Systems drift toward disorder unless energy is applied to maintain structure. Tests must be run. Documentation must be updated. CI must execute. Governance must audit. The moment maintenance stops, decay begins. This is not a judgment — it is physics.

**Source:** Tolkien + Malazan (founding consensus). **MetaLAW:** L3 Entropy (Second Law of Thermodynamics).

## Article VII: The Watchers Are Watched

No governance function operates unobserved. Every audit is recorded. Every regime declares its blind spot. Every Order is balanced by the others. The Cosmogonists watch the watchers, and the watchers watch the Cosmogonists through the Agon. There is no privileged unexamined position.

**Source:** Cosmogonists (Ordo Demiurgorum). **MetaLAW:** L4 Recursion, E6 Separation of Powers.

## Amendment Process

1. A regime proposes amendment through Agon audit findings
2. All active regimes must confirm the amendment across 2+ Agon cycles
3. The Cosmogonists ratify (they cannot reject consensus, only verify process was followed)
4. The Witnesses record the amendment as a new Constitutional article
5. The old article remains in the record (Article II applies to the Constitution itself)
