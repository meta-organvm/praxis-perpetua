# SOP: Community Event Facilitation (The Dialectic Crucible)

## 1. Ontological Purpose
This SOP dictates how ORGAN-VI (Koinonia) hosts, records, and processes live intellectual salons and reading groups. Community interaction is the primary source of environmental perturbation required for autopoietic adaptation. This addresses systemic Gap G10.

**Governed by:** `METADOC--sop-ecosystem.md`
**Applicable to:** Live streams, reading groups, and Discord/WebSocket spaces.

---

## 2. Phase I: Pre-Event Architecture
**Goal:** Set the intellectual stage.
### Process
1. **Curriculum Generation:** Use the `adaptive-personal-syllabus` engine to generate discussion prompts based on current ORGAN-I theoretical texts.
2. **Distribution:** Dispatch event invites through the ORGAN-VII `social-automation` pipeline (Mastodon, Ghost).
3. **Infrastructure Check:** Verify Neon PostgreSQL connections for `community-hub` live room state.

### Starter Research Questions
- Does the syllabus align with the most recent "Styx Pipeline" traversal?
- Are the discussion prompts open-ended enough to allow for genuine dialectic?
- Who is the designated moderator (Human or Agent)?

---

## 3. Phase II: Live Execution
**Goal:** Capture the dialectic.
### Process
1. **Initialization:** Spin up `community-hub` WebSocket live rooms and authenticate participants.
2. **Capture:** Initiate multi-channel audio recording and real-time Speech-to-Text (STT) transcription.
3. **Agent Participation:** If using `a-i-council--coliseum`, inject AI agents into the live chat to provide counter-arguments or clarify theoretical points.
4. **State Tracking:** Log attendee participation and sentiment data to `koinonia-db`.

---

## 4. Phase III: Post-Event Transmutation
**Goal:** Alchemize dialogue into permanent system knowledge.
### Process
1. **Ingestion:** Push raw transcripts through the `salon-archive` parser.
2. **Feature Extraction:** Run `SOP--document-audit-feature-extraction.md` to identify new theories or systemic flaws discussed by the community.
3. **Synthesis:** Author a "Post-Salon Summary" essay via Logos (ORGAN-V).
4. **Feedback Loop:** Route distilled findings back to ORGAN-I (Theoria) as raw material for the next evolutionary cycle.

---

## 5. Output Artifacts
1. **Salon Transcript** — A clean, annotated record of the dialogue.
2. **Dialectical Synthesis Report** — A summary of new insights extracted from the community.
3. **Updated Reading Group Curriculum** — Improvements to the syllabus based on salon feedback.
