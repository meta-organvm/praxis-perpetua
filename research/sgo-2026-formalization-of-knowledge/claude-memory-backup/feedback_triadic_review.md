**RULE: Three points of view, always.** Not one agreeing with itself. Not two in binary debate. Three creating a harmonious system of critique, expansion, amendment, and divergence.

**Why:** One POV produces echo. Two produce debate (thesis/antithesis without synthesis). Three produce triangulation — structural integrity through non-redundant coverage. This is consistent with Peirce's triadic sign model, psychometric inter-rater agreement (minimum 3 for reliable ICC), and the Governance Trilemma (three axes of evaluation).

**How to apply:**
1. Every paper, implementation, publication, or governance change gets 3 POVs before advancing.
2. The three POVs are AMORPHOUS — constituted per-venture based on its domain signature. The container shapes the liquid.
3. Distribution: one sympathetic, one adversarial, one orthogonal. Always this distribution.
4. Each POV must produce substantive output: critique, expansion, amendment, or fork. No bare approval.
5. Resolution: 3/3 advance → advance. 2/3 advance → advance with amendments. 1/3 advance → revise. 0/3 → major revision.
6. The SOP lives at: `intake/research-adventures-2026-03/SOP-TRIADIC-REVIEW-PROTOCOL.md` and is backed up in the SGO corpus.
