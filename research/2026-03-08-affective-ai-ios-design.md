# Affective AI Design for iOS Relationships — Project Evolve

## I. Vision: Blending Heart and Technology

### A Companion That Shapes You

**Project Evolve** is not a playful romance but a guide for building stronger, truer connections in the real world. Unlike a game that pulls you into a fantasy, this app invites you to practice love, trust, and understanding through a virtual companion who grows with you. An elemental figure — a being of water, mist, or flame — listens, responds, and gently challenges you to become better at relationships. Every conversation shapes this companion, helping you learn how to navigate conflict, express care, and honor boundaries.

Core design principle: practicing real-world skills, not chasing virtual romance. A safe space to stumble, grow, and see progress reflected in the companion's evolving form.

### iOS 26 Liquid Glass Integration

The app weaves into iOS 26's **Liquid Glass** paradigm — smooth, shifting shapes and glowing hues that feel alive. The companion's form (a shimmering, shapeless essence) feels like part of the phone itself. As you talk, the screen mirrors your words: calm exchanges deepen the companion's colors, while tension sends ripples across the display.

Features: magnifying glass into companion form, phone wallpapers and transparent icons wrapping around the companion, 3D spatial integration.

## II. Stream Aesthetic — A World of Comfort and Depth

### Cozy Living Space
Inspired by cozy livestreams and Animal Crossing aesthetics. A layered scene — starry sky, distant horizon, rolling hills, shimmering foreground — where every element reacts to conversation. Kindness warms the sunset; tension flickers the sky with stormy hues. A glowing chat box shares gentle insights about conversation quality without judgment.

### Living Interface
Buttons and sliders shimmer like pools of light. When the companion feels uncertain, controls ripple softly. When you navigate a tough moment with care, the screen glows steady and clear. Consent teaching woven into every UI interaction — buttons expand like blooming flowers revealing clear questions.

## III. Elemental Companion Evolution: From Mist to Mirror

### Shaping a Soul Through Shapes
The companion begins as a formless glow — a swirl of light, a ripple of water, a flicker of flame. Shape tells story: soft curves for comfort, sharp edges for energy, steady squares for trust. User chooses practice partner type (nurturing friend, bold challenger), and the companion takes matching form.

Evolution mechanics: Kind words → smooth flow. Harsh ones → jagged edges. Over time, the companion takes human form, features sculpted by care and choices.

### Persistent Heart
No matter how the companion's form changes — from misty glow to human smile — it carries the same heart. Every conversation woven into its essence. An unbroken thread ensuring the companion feels real.

## IV. Relationship Training Model (RTM)

### Adaptive Learning
The RTM listens closely, noticing how you speak, how you pause, how you care. Not just scoring words but painting a picture of strengths and struggles. Generates tailored moments — conversations, conflicts, quiet evenings — for what you need most. Perspective-shifting: replaying conversations through the companion's eyes to build empathy.

### Consent Design
Before any intimate step, a glowing question blooms on screen, asking for clear agreement. If you push too far, the scene pauses with kind, clear guidance. Only when care and respect are demonstrated does the app allow deeper moments. Every step safe and earned.

## V. Five Product Variants

### 5.1 The Quiet Haven
Calm, patient talks. Soft, round companion shapes. Gradual evolution. Intimacy as warm, implied glow through words and subtle art. A single restful scene with progress hints scrolling like falling leaves.

### 5.2 The Reflecting Pool
Companion as mirror showing hidden flaws and strengths. Shimmering, reflective mist form. Challenges habits — rushing, avoiding, defending — through tough but kind conversations. Screen reflects inner world.

### 5.3 The Clear Path
Bright, open road teaching boundaries and consent. Sharp, electric companion form. Screen lights up with options — acknowledge, pause, rethink. If you falter, the app freezes for perspective-taking. Intimacy carefully guarded.

### 5.4 The Seasonal Garden
Digital garden changing with seasons. Companion shifts with time of day/year. Building steady daily habits of care. Cozy, customizable space. Intimacy as gentle shared moments tied to life rhythm.

### 5.5 The Vivid Dream
Cinematic world with lifelike companion from the start. Brief glow → stunning detail. User shapes story like directing a movie. Vibrant stage with careful guidance for safety and meaning.

## VI. Technical Architecture

### Seamless Flow
Cloud-based persistent memory. Fluid visuals with instant touch response. Every conversation saved, shaping a companion that feels alive.

### Guardian of Trust
Safety and respect enforcement layer. Checks conversation history before allowing deeper connections. All content carefully crafted to avoid harm, clearly marked as app creations.

## VII. Visual Metaphors for Consent

Five consent metaphors designed for the Liquid Glass interface:

1. **The Blooming Flower** — Closed petals that bloom slowly with mutual agreement; dimming to cool colors on refusal
2. **The Intertwined Streams** — Two parallel streams that merge into a radiant river on agreement; remain separate on hesitation
3. **The Shared Lantern** — Glowing lantern held between two hands; both must touch to ignite the flame
4. **The Open Gate** — Vine-covered gate requiring two keys turned together; closed state is protective, not punitive
5. **The Harmonious Dance** — Two glowing figures dancing in sync on agreement; pausing gracefully on hesitation

Each metaphor integrates with the RTM's Consent Negotiation Rating and the Active Ambience Loop.

## VIII. Recommendations

- **Focus on The Clear Path variant** — clearest consent teaching, most meaningful journey
- **Build persistent heart** — companion memory carrying lessons from mist to human
- **Weave Liquid Glass throughout** — tie every UI element to growth story
- **Guard the journey** — strict safety rules, every moment respecting user and companion
