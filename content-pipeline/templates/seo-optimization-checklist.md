## Thumbnail

- [ ] **Dimensions:** 1280 x 720 pixels, 16:9 aspect ratio
- [ ] **File size:** Under 2MB
- [ ] **Object is the visual hero** -- dramatic lighting, cinematic color grading, object fills significant frame area
- [ ] **Text overlay:** 3-5 words of bold text -- question or declaration derived from title
- [ ] Text is high-contrast and readable at mobile thumbnail size (test at 120x68px preview)
- [ ] **Color grading** is consistent with Object Lessons visual template (cinematic, not YouTube-generic)
- [ ] No clickbait faces, arrows, circles, or emoji overlays -- the aesthetic is cinema, not reaction content
- [ ] Thumbnail tells a story even without the title (the object + text should communicate the concept)
- [ ] **A/B variant** created with different text or framing for Test & Compare

## Chapter Markers

- [ ] **First timestamp at 0:00** (required by YouTube to enable chapters)
- [ ] Minimum 3 chapters, no maximum (but don't over-segment -- 5-8 is ideal for 10-12 min videos)
- [ ] **Labels are keyword-rich** and descriptive:
  - Good: "Milk as Innocence -- A Clockwork Orange to Get Out"
  - Bad: "Example 1" or "Part 2"
- [ ] Chapter labels are structured by theme/meaning, not just chronological order
- [ ] Each chapter label makes sense as a standalone search result (YouTube surfaces chapters in search)
- [ ] Timestamp format: `0:00`, `1:23`, `10:45` (no leading zeros on minutes)

**Chapters:**

```
0:00 [opening chapter label]
```

## End Screens and Cards

### End Screens (last 20 seconds of video)

- [ ] 2-3 end screen elements configured:
  - [ ] **Best for viewer** or specific video recommendation (next Object Lesson or most relevant)
  - [ ] **Subscribe button** positioned visibly
  - [ ] **Playlist link** (Object Lessons playlist)
- [ ] End screen elements do not overlap with important visual content
- [ ] Video content accommodates end screen placement (clean background or dedicated end slate)

- [ ] 1-2 cards placed at relevant moments linking to related Object Lessons episodes
- [ ] Cards appear when the narration references or connects to another episode's subject
- [ ] No cards in the first 30 seconds (let viewers settle in)

## Shorts Strategy

- [ ] **3-5 Shorts** extracted per episode
- [ ] Each Short is a self-contained moment (compelling without needing the full video)
- [ ] Shorts are vertical (9:16) or square (1:1) -- not letterboxed horizontal footage
- [ ] **Text overlay** on each Short: "Full video on my channel" or "Link in pinned comment"
- [ ] **Pinned comment** on each Short links directly to the full episode
- [ ] Shorts scheduled across the following 2-3 weeks (1-2 per week, not all at once)
- [ ] Shorts titles are keyword-rich and hook-driven (different from main video title)
- [ ] Shorts descriptions include main video link and 1-2 hashtags
